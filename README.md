Sankat Sewa

Connecting People to Help When It Matters Most

Sankat Sewa is a smart incident reporting and emergency response platform designed for Nepal. The app allows citizens to instantly report accidents, disasters, and emergencies, while automatically notifying nearby volunteers and relevant local authorities for rapid response.

Whether it’s a road accident, fire, flood, landslide, or medical emergency, Sankat Sewa bridges the gap between those in need and those ready to help.

🚨 Key Features

📍 Real-time incident reporting with location tracking

👥 Smart volunteer allocation based on proximity

🏛 Direct alert system for local authorities

📊 Incident dashboard for monitoring and response tracking

🔔 Instant notifications for faster action

🌍 Why Sankat Sewa?

Nepal faces frequent natural disasters and emergency situations. Delays in communication often cost valuable time. Sankat Sewa creates a coordinated digital network between citizens, volunteers, and authorities — ensuring faster response, better coordination, and safer communities.
